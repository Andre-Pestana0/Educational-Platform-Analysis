**Hoare‑logic specification**

Let  

\[
S \;:=\; \sum_{k=0}^{19}\frac{2k+1}{2^{\,k}}
\qquad\text{(the exact mathematical value required by the problem).}
\]

Denote by `out` the string that the program writes to the standard output.  
The function  

\[
\operatorname{fmt}_{2}(x)\;=\;\frac{\big\lfloor 100\,x+ \tfrac12\big\rfloor}{100}
\]

produces the real number obtained by rounding \(x\) to two decimal places (the usual “printf‑%.2f” behaviour).  

---

### Pre‑condition  

\[
\boxed{P \;=\; \top}
\]

There is no input; the program may start in any state.

---

### Post‑condition  

\[
\boxed{Q \;=\; \text{out is the decimal representation of } 
\operatorname{fmt}_{2}(S)
\text{ with exactly two digits after the decimal point.}}
\]

Equivalently, in logical form:

\[
Q \;:\; \exists\,v\in\mathbb{R}\;.\;
\Bigl(v = \operatorname{fmt}_{2}(S)\Bigr)\;\land\;
\bigl(\text{out} = \text{string\_of}(v)\bigr),
\]

where `string_of(v)` is the usual conversion of a real number to a character
sequence that shows exactly two fractional digits (e.g. “12.34”).

---

### Hoare triple for the whole algorithm  

\[
\boxed{\;\{\,\top\,\}\;\; \text{Algorithm} \;\; \{\,\text{out = fmt}_{2}(S)\,\}\;}
\]

Thus the algorithm must compute the finite sum  

\[
S = \sum_{k=0}^{19}\frac{2k+1}{2^{k}}
\]

and print it rounded to two decimal places, satisfying the post‑condition above.
