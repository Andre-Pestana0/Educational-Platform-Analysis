Below is a **pure Hoare‑logic specification** (pre‑condition / post‑condition) written only with mathematical notation.  
No implementation details (loops, I/O calls, etc.) are shown – the specification tells *what* the program must achieve.

---

### 1. Vocabulary  

* `ℕ` – the set of natural numbers (0,1,2,…).  
* `N ∈ ℕ` – the integer read from the input.  
* `out` – the (finite) string that the program finally writes to the standard output.  
* `·` – string concatenation (associative, left‑to‑right).  
* `"Ho"` – the one‑character‑token **Ho**.  
* `" "` – the one‑character‑token space.  
* `"!"` – the exclamation‑mark token.  

For a natural number `k ≥ 1` we define the *repetition* operator  

\[
\operatorname{rep}_k(s) \;=\;
\underbrace{s\;·\;s\;·\;\dots\;·\;s}_{k\text{ copies}}
\]

and the *space‑separated repetition*  

\[
\operatorname{sprep}_k(s) \;=\;
\begin{cases}
s                     & k = 1\\[2mm]
s\;·\;" "\;·\;\operatorname{sprep}_{k-1}(s) & k > 1 .
\end{cases}
\]

Thus  

\[
\operatorname{sprep}_k("Ho") = 
\underbrace{"Ho"\; " "\; "Ho"\; " "\;\dots\; " "\; "Ho"}_{k\text{ copies of “Ho” separated by a single space}} .
\]

---

### 2. Hoare triple  

\[
\boxed{\;\{\,P\,\}\;\; S\;\;\{\,Q\,\}\;}
\]

*`S`* denotes the whole program (read `N`, produce the required output).

#### Pre‑condition  **P**

\[
P \;\equiv\; \bigl( N \in \mathbb{N}\ \wedge\ 0 < N \le 10^{6} \bigr)
\]

The only requirement on the input is that it be a positive integer not larger than \(10^{6}\).

#### Post‑condition **Q**

\[
\begin{aligned}
Q \;\equiv\; &\; out = \bigl(\operatorname{sprep}_{N}("Ho")\bigr) \;·\; "!" \\[2mm]
           &\;\;\;\wedge\; \bigl|\,\operatorname{sprep}_{N}("Ho")\,\bigr| = 
               2N-1 \quad\text{(character length before the final “!”)}\\[2mm]
           &\;\;\;\wedge\; \forall i\in\{1,\dots,N\}\;:\;
                \text{the }i\text{-th token of }out\text{ (ignoring the final “!”)} 
                = "Ho" .
\end{aligned}
\]

In words:

* the output string consists of exactly **N** copies of the word **Ho**, each pair of consecutive copies being separated by a single space,
* after the last copy of **Ho** an exclamation mark **!** is appended,
* no other characters appear.

---

### 3. Full specification in Hoare‑logic form  

\[
\boxed{
\bigl\{\,0 < N \le 10^{6}\,\bigr\}
\;S\;
\bigl\{
   out = \operatorname{sprep}_{N}("Ho")·"!"
\;\}
}
\]

That is the complete formal (pre/post) specification of the “Santa‑Ho” problem.
