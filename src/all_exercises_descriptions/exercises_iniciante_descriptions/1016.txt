Description: Two cars (X and Y) leave in the same direction. The car X leaves with a constant speed of 60 km/h and the car Y leaves with a constant speed of 90 km / h.

Input: The input file contains 1 integer value.

Output: Print the necessary time followed by the message "minutos" that means minutes in Portuguese.

Examples:
- Example 1:
  Input:
30
  Output:
60 minutos

- Example 2:
  Input:
110
  Output:
220 minutos

- Example 3:
  Input:
7
  Output:
14 minutos
