Description: Bilbo Baggins lived in a hole in the ground in Vila dos Hobbits. One day, your friend Gandalf, invites you on an adventure. The adventure was based on stealing a dragon's treasure. Bilbo soon thought it would be madness to steal a dragon, but Gandalf had an entourage of dwarves with him to assist him in this toil.

Input: The single line at the entrance contains 2 integers, N and X, indicating respectively the number of dwarves that Gandalf managed to help in the adventure and the distance from Vila dos Hobbits to the Lonely Mountain.

Output: A real number with two decimal places indicating the number of days to reach the Lonely Mountain.

Examples:
- Example 1:
  Input:
2 4
  Output:
1.00

- Example 2:
  Input:
13 1000
  Output:
66.67
