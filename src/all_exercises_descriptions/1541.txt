Description: Mr Pi is a famous constructor of Codingland. He needs your help to find out the best lands in the city to his projects.

Input: The input has many test cases. Each one is made by three integer numbersA,BandC(> 0 and <= 1000) separated by one blank space. This numbers are the size of the house (AandB) and the maximum percentage allowed to build in this neighborhood (C). An unique 0 (zero) value means end of inputs.

Output: Should be printed an integer number, that is the size of the land. This value should be truncated.

Examples:
- Example 1:
  Input:
8 10 20
1 10 100
10 3 100
0
  Output:
20
3
5
